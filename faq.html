Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common troubleshooting questions.

How do I know if my PC has a virus?

Look for unusual popups, high CPU usage when idle, unknown apps, and browser redirects. Run a full scan with Windows Defender or a trusted antivirus.

Should I upgrade RAM or storage first?

If your PC is slow to boot and open apps, an SSD upgrade is most impactful. If apps/browser tabs crash or freeze, upgrade RAM.

Is it safe to clean my laptop fan?

Yes, if done carefully. Use compressed air through vents. For internal cleaning, disconnect power and follow a model-specific guide.

How often should I defragment?

Only for HDDs and only occasionally. Do not defragment SSDs; they use TRIM and wear-leveling.

What is the simplest way to speed up Windows?

Disable startup apps, uninstall bloatware, install Windows on an SSD, and keep drivers updated.
